FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Real questions we get from investors and institutions ‚Äî answered honestly.

How does Fastricity survive as a business? +
Revenue comes from energy sales to the grid, corporate wellness partnerships, municipal contracts, and brand sponsorships on our stations. The reward points are subsidised by the value of energy generated ‚Äî as ridership scales, margins improve significantly.
What is the backup plan if ridership is low? +
Stations are deployed with gamification features, campus challenges, and corporate tie-ins that drive guaranteed baseline usage. In parallel, our stations double as smart EV charging points during off-peak hours, ensuring revenue continuity regardless of cycle usage.
When does Fastricity break even? +
Our model projects break-even at ~150 active stations with 80 rides/day per station. At our current growth rate and with pilot data validating unit economics, we project break-even by Q3 of Year 2 post-seed funding.
Are you partnering with any institutions? +
Yes! We are in active MoU discussions with 3 state universities, 2 IT parks, and a municipal corporation. Institutional partnerships fast-track adoption by guaranteeing user volume and sharing station capital costs.

Process

How We Work

Five elegant steps from picking up a cycle to earning your rewards.

üìç Step 1 ‚Äî Pick Up a Cycle

Scan your Fastricity QR code at any nearby station. Your app unlocks a cycle automatically ‚Äî no cash, no card needed. Just show up and ride.

1
2

‚ö° Step 2 ‚Äî Pedal & Generate Energy

An in-cycle piezoelectric + dynamo system converts your kinetic energy into electrical current. The harder you pedal, the more you generate.

üîã Step 3 ‚Äî Energy Stored in Battery

Generated electricity is stored in a smart lithium-iron battery pack embedded in the station dock. A BMS ensures safe, optimised charging at all times.

3
4

üåê Step 4 ‚Äî Transferred to Main Grid

When the battery reaches capacity, a micro-inverter converts DC to AC. Energy flows into the distribution grid through our licensed utility partnership.

üèÜ Step 5 ‚Äî User Earns Points

Energy units are logged against your profile. Points are credited instantly ‚Äî visible in your app, redeemable for rides, vouchers, and transit passes.
